# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful, welcoming environment for everyone who wants to improve PokeNav.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- being constructive and respectful in feedback
- grounding disagreements in product goals or evidence
- helping contributors understand the data model and browse philosophy
- keeping discussions collaborative, especially around partial or ambiguous source data

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- doxxing or publishing private information
- bad-faith disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Repeated or serious violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, release notes, and any public spaces used to represent this project.
